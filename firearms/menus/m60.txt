Credits: 18
Type: General Purpose Machine Gun
Caliber: 7.62x51mm NATO
Capacity: Belt-Fed, 100 Round Boxes

Description:
When used prone to take advantage of its
bipod, the M60 is a highly effective weapon,
capable of turning any area into a lethal
kill zone. Feeding from a 100 round belt,
it makes an excellent choice for providing
cover or suppressive fire for a squad.
