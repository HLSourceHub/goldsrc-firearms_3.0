Type: Light Machine Gun
Caliber: 5.56x45mm NATO
Capacity: Belt-Fed, 200 Round Boxes

Description:
Although the M249 SAW, or Minimi, fires a
considerably smaller caliber round than
the M60, it is equally capable of creating
an effective base of fire thanks to its 
extreme cyclic rate. This has made it a
popular support weapon around the world.
