Credits: 13
Type: Assault Rifle
Caliber: 5.56x45mm NATO
Capacity: 25 Round Box Magazine
ROF: 1100 Rounds Per Minute
Description:
Jokingly called "le Clarion" (the Bugle)
by the French troopers issued with it, the
FA-MAS' very high cyclic rate makes it
difficult to control on full-auto, but gives
it a lethal advantage at close range.
