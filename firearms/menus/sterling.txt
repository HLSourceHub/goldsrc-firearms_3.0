Name: Sterling L2A3MK4
Credits: 8
Type: Sub Machinegun
Caliber: 9x19mm NATO
Capacity: 34 Round Box Magazine
ROF: 800 Rounds Per Minute
Description:
This lightweight, fast-firing weapon evolved
from the WWII Sten. Although not very
accurate, its high ROF makes it useful in
close quarters. Often chosen as a backup
weapon for snipers who may find themselves
closer to their enemies than they had planned.
