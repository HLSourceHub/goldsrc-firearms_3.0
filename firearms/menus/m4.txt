                                             
Name: M4 Carbine
Credits: 13
Type: Assault Rifle
Caliber: 5.56x45mm NATO
Capacity: 30 Round Box Magazine
ROF: 750 Rounds Per Minute
Description:
The M4 carbine is effectively an M16A2 with
a shorter barrel and telescopic stock.
Due to its shorter length and fully automatic
capabilities, it has become a favorite for
close quarters urban combat for both military
and law enforcement groups.

[To suppress this weapon, you must have
the "Stealth" skill.  Secondary fire attaches
and detatches the suppressor.]
