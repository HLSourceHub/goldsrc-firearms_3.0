                                             
Name: PKM
Credits: 17
Type: General Purpose Machine Gun
Caliber: 7.62x54mm Rimmed
Capacity: Belt-Ded, 100 Round Boxes
ROF: 700 Rounds Per Minute
Description:
With a high ROF and a heavy-caliber round,
the PKM provides a great deal of firepower
in a very short period of time, at the expense
of running through ammunition very quickly.
