This classifies you as a Field Medic. This skill is a line of sight
skill meaning that if you perform an ability, it will take effect
on the player standing directly in front of you. If there are no
other players in front of you, the ability is used upon yourself.
All abilities require a bandage (considered medical supplies) in
the inventory for the ability to take affect. This skill grants you
3 abilities to perform and an additional 4 bandages. The abilities
are:

   *Treat (useskill treat) - Mend legs, restore 25 health, MEDEVAC
          casualties, bandaging and give bandage.
   *Adrenaline (useskill adrenaline) - Restore stamina.
   *Set Hospice (useskill hospice) - Drop a hospice flag.
