                                             
Name: Anaconda
Credits: 4
Type: Revolver
Caliber: .44 Magnum
Capacity: 6 Round Cylinder
ROF: Double Action.
Description:
Chambered for the powerful .44 Magnum,
this heavy revolver is powerful and
accurate. Many shooters choose to mount
a scope for extra precision on long-range
shots.

[To use the scope on the Anaconda, you
must choose the "Marksmanship I" skill.]
