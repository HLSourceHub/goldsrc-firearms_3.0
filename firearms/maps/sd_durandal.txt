DURANDAL (Search and Destroy)

by David Reeves (Hitman)

RED TEAM: Target crates have stenciled M60
and M16 markings on their tops. Use of
explosives recommended to destroy crates.

BLUE TEAM: You are part of a NATO 
Task Force. Defend your supply crates and
destroy the enemy crates. Your targets
will be marked with FA-MAS and missle
symbols on the top. Use of explosives
recommended for this task.
