-== Objective==-

Blue Team: 
The United States has actively invaded your small 
city in South America. Organize your army regulars 
to defend your home from these foreign conquerers.
Capture the city and push the United States marines
out of the city.

Red Team:
Too long has corruption and greed gripped the throats
of innocent lifes in South America. Join the crusade
to destroy the warlords of South America. Capture 
the whole city and recieve for reinforcements.
